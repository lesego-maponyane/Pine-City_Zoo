LION

Lions are large, powerful cats (Panthera leo) known as the "king of the jungle." They are apex predators with muscular bodies, short legs, and sharp claws. Male lions are easily recognizable by their prominent mane, a distinctive feature not found in female lions.

Size and Appearance: Lions are large, powerful cats with a muscular build. Male lions are larger than females and possess a mane. Habitat: They are native to Sub-Saharan Africa and India. Social Structure: Lions live in groups called prides, with female lions typically doing the hunting.

Hunting: They are apex predators, hunting a variety of large prey. Mane: Male lions have a mane of hair around their neck, which can vary in color and length. Roar: Lions are known for their loud roars, which can be heard over long distances.
